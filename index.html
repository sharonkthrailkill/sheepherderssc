What We Do

We meet veterans where they are and help remove barriers to safety and stability.

Street Outreach

We locate veterans who are unsheltered and build trust through consistent, respectful support.

  • Wellness and safety check-ins
  • Basic needs and connection points
  • Follow-up and case coordination

Shelter Placement

We work to connect veterans to safe, clean environments and appropriate programs.

  • Short-term shelter referrals
  • Transportation coordination (when available)
  • Support navigating next steps

Resource Navigation

We help connect veterans to local partners and services that support long-term stability.

  • Benefits guidance and referrals
  • Healthcare and recovery resources
  • ID/doc support pathways

How It Works

A simple process built around safety, dignity, and follow-through.

  1. 1

    Reach out

    Contact us if you’re a veteran who needs help—or if you’ve seen a veteran who may be at risk.

  2. 2

    Locate & connect

    We attempt to locate the veteran, make contact, and offer support based on their needs and consent.

  3. 3

    Move toward safety

    When possible, we coordinate placement or referrals and stay connected to help reduce drop-off.
