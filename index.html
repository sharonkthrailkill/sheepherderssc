What We Do

Sheep Herders Homeless Veteran Outreach Service connects honorably discharged veterans experiencing homelessness with the resources they need to reach safety and stability. Through direct outreach, intake assistance, and coordination with VA and trusted community partners, we help veterans move from crisis into clean, secure environments.

We connect directly with veterans in crisis and help begin the path toward stability.

  • Community outreach to locate veterans in need
  • One-on-one intake and documentation support
  • Assistance beginning VA and housing enrollment
  • Ongoing support until safe placement is secured

We help veterans navigate medical and housing systems.

  • Coordination with William Jennings Bryan Dorn VA
  • Non-emergency transportation to VA appointments
  • Navigation of medical and benefit resources
  • Connection to trusted community partner organizations

We provide essential items during crisis stabilization.

  • Hygiene kits and basic necessities
  • Clothing assistance
  • Immediate support items during stabilization
  • Supplemental aid while awaiting placement

About Us

Mission Statement: Sheep Herders of South Carolina Homeless Veteran Outreach Service is committed to finding and supporting veterans experiencing homelessness, guiding them out of dangerous situations and into safe, clean environments—because those who served our country should never be forgotten.

We meet veterans where they are and help navigate the next right step—paperwork, appointments, and safe placement. Our approach is hands-on, respectful, and built on trusted connections.

  • Respect and dignity in every interaction
  • Hands-on help with intake and documentation
  • Coordination with VA and community partners
  • Consistent support through the placement process

We believe outreach should be consistent, respectful, and practical.

  • Dignity first — Every veteran deserves to be seen and treated with respect.
  • Action over words — We focus on real steps that move someone toward safety.
  • Partnership matters — We collaborate with trusted agencies to strengthen outcomes.
  • No veteran left behind — We follow through until safe placement is secured.

We exist to locate honorably discharged veterans experiencing homelessness and connect them to safe, stable housing through direct outreach and coordinated support.

  • Service Area: Richland, Orangeburg, Spartanburg, Aiken, Lexington, and Sumter counties in South Carolina.
  • Who We Serve: Honorably discharged veterans in need of housing stabilization support.
